JSt

JSt


is a browser fullscreen video player, that works on every page you can find a video. It is designed with use in mind and that is why you should give it a try. Let me introduce you to JSt...

No need to install.


Just drag this JSt onto your bookmarks panel. When you are ready to rock and roll - click this bookmark, magic will happen automatically.

Watch with friends.


On both devices set the timeout to start watching the video simultaneously, JSt will do the rest :-) .
